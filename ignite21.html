About IGNITE 2021

IGNITE 2021 is the Third edition of the Annual Sports Meet of IGDTUW. IGNITE is a pioneering force in the domain of all-women organized Sports Meets in Delhi. We at IGDTUW, believe that there is an unexplored niche when it comes to celebrations of sports and sportspersons, and of female involvement in sports. Our aim is to create our mark here, and to successfully organize a 2-day sports extravaganza. The event will be graced by many achievers in the field of sports, as well as by teams from colleges across India.
